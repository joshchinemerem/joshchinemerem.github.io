// 01 — ABOUT

From the stockroom floor to the dashboard.

My path into data started in inventory and procurement, not a classroom — auditing stock, reconciling purchase orders against invoices, and chasing down why the numbers didn't match. That habit of distrusting a number until it's verified is still how I approach every dataset.

Today I work as a Supply Chain Analyst at Masters Energy Group and remotely as a Purchase Analyst at Nenye Farm, building dashboards and forecasts that procurement and finance teams actually use to make calls. In parallel, I'm formalizing the statistical and machine learning side of that intuition through an MSc in Statistics and an applied ML/AI programme.

I'm most interested in roles where messy, real-world operational data (inventory, purchasing, logistics) needs to be turned into something a non-technical stakeholder can trust and act on.
